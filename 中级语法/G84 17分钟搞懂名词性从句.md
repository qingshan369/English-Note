|:---------------:|:---------------------------------------------------------------------:|:------------:|
|   连接代词(n)   | what、which、who、whom、whatever、whichever、whoever、whomever、where | 作主、宾、表 |

| 连接形容词(adj) |          what、which、whose、whatever、whichever、whoseever           |     作定     |
